VibeDeck Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2, 2026

Overview

VibeDeck is an iOS music player and audio control app for importing, organizing, analyzing, and playing audio files on your device.

Personal Data

VibeDeck does not require an account and does not sell personal information. The app is intended to work with audio files that you choose to import.

Audio Files

Audio files and track metadata are used to provide playback, waveform, queue, analysis, pitch, filter, VibeMod, and equalizer features. Imported files remain under your control unless you export, move, or share them using iOS features.

Analytics, Advertising, And Tracking

VibeDeck does not include third-party advertising or cross-app tracking. If analytics or crash reporting tools are added in a future version, this policy will be updated before release.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact hello@vibedeckplayer.app.
