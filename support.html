VibeDeck Support

Help for importing tracks, managing the queue, playback, EQ, and sound controls.

Contact

For support, questions, or bug reports, contact hello@vibedeckplayer.app.

Importing Tracks

VibeDeck can work with audio files you choose from iOS Files, iCloud Drive, Downloads, AirDrop, and supported share sources.

Queue And Playback

The queue helps prepare sets, review current and upcoming tracks, search library items, and keep playback context visible.

Sound Tools

VibeDeck includes pitch/filter control, EQ, VibeMod, limiter, mono/side options, waveform display, and transport controls.
